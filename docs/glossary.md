# Glossary of DeFi Terms

- **APR (Annual Percentage Rate)**: The yearly interest rate expressed as a percentage, used to calculate the cost of borrowing or the return on lending.
- **APY (Annual Percentage Yield)**: The effective annual rate of return taking into account compound interest.
- **Collateral**: An asset pledged by the borrower to secure a loan; it can be seized if repayment defaults.
- **Liquidation**: The process of selling the collateral automatically when its value falls below a required threshold.
- **Utilization**: The proportion of available liquidity that is currently lent out, expressed as a percentage.
- **Daily Earnings**: The amount earned each day based on the APR/APY and the amount lent.
- **Total Earnings**: The cumulative earnings over the selected loan period.
- **Monthly Payment**: The amount the borrower must pay each month to repay the loan.
- **Total Repayment**: The overall amount repaid over the loan's lifetime, including principal and interest.
- **Total Interest**: The total interest accrued over the loan period.
- **Daily Interest**: Interest earned or accrued each day.
